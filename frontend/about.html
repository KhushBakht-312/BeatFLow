About BeatFlow

BeatFlow is an AI-powered heartbeat analysis system. It detects healthy
and unhealthy heartbeats and helps users understand their ECG data.

The system analyzes ECG signals, detects R-peaks, calculates
heart rate variability, and classifies heartbeats as healthy or unhealthy.

With BeatFlow, you can monitor heart health. The intuitive interface
allows anyone to upload ECG data and get instant AI-powered feedback.
